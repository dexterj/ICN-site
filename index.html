Indie Comic Ninja

The Podcast

Indie Comic Ninja is a podcast of interviews with comic book creators with a focus on independent and small press. In the past I've interviewd small time creators from the mid-west to "big time" Kickstarter types from the west coast and even had some episodes about super fans with advice on how to talk to creators and get commissions. I (oh hello, I'm Dexter J.) am an aspiring writer and collector of comic books and comic book art with a history.
